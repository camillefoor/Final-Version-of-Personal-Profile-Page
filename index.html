My Story

I am currently a Treehouse Techdegree student and aspiring web designer. I love what I am learning and am excited to expand my skill set. I've lived all over the world and have experienced many different cultures and aesthetics that I look forward to using in my design work.

I grew up on a farm in a small town in northern California. My travels and work have allowed me to spend time in LA, The Netherlands, England, Russia, China, and Louisville, Kentucky. Now, I feel like I have made it full circle, returning to my small town roots by moving to Big Rapids, Michigan with my husband, Josh, last year. In addition to my web design skills, my personal experiences and wide exposure to the world will enable me to add the essential human touch necessary to creating compelling web designs.
